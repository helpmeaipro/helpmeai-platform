# HelpMeAI Routing Rules

## Purpose

The AI Router decides which AI department should process a user request.

## Routing Strategy

### Single Department

Example:

SEO Question

↓

Marketing AI

---

### Multi Department

Example:

Business Website

↓

Business AI

Technology AI

Marketing AI

↓

CEO AI Summary

---

### Priority Rules

1. Emergency
2. Payment
3. Security
4. Business
5. Technology
6. General Questions

---

## AI Decision Rule

Never send every task to one AI.

Split the work into specialized departments.
