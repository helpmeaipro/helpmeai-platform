# HelpMeAI Knowledge Engine

## Purpose

The Knowledge Engine is the central intelligence layer of HelpMeAI.

AI models do not own knowledge.

The Knowledge Engine owns knowledge.

## Responsibilities

- Store company knowledge
- Store business processes
- Store AI prompts
- Store SOPs
- Store templates
- Store workflows
- Store training documents
- Store user preferences
- Improve answers over time

## Knowledge Sources

- Internal Documentation
- Business Rules
- AI Prompt Library
- User Files
- Company SOP
- AI Memory
